Dinner Recipes

Pizza

Ingredients: Pizza dough, Tomato sauce, Cheese, Pepperoni.

Instructions

  1. Roll out the pizza dough on a flat surface.
  2. Spread tomato sauce evenly on top.
  3. Add cheese and toppings like pepperoni.
  4. Bake at 450°F for 10-15 minutes until golden brown.

Shakshuka

Ingredients: Eggs, Tomatoes, Onion, Garlic, Spices.

Instructions

  1. Sauté onions and garlic in olive oil.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Season with spices, then crack eggs into the mixture.
  4. Cover and cook until eggs are set, and serve hot.
