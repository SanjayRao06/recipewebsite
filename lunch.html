Lunch Recipes

Pasta

Ingredients: Pasta, Tomato sauce, Olive oil, Garlic, Basil.

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Mix pasta into the sauce, garnish with basil, and serve.

Spaghetti

Ingredients: Spaghetti, Olive oil, Garlic, Parmesan cheese.

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in boiling water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil and garlic in a pan.
  3. Mix cooked spaghetti into the pan and toss well.
  4. Top with Parmesan cheese and serve.
