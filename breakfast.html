Breakfast Recipes

Dosa

Ingredients: Rice flour, Urad dal, Water, Salt.

Instructions

  1. Soak urad dal for 4-5 hours.
  2. Grind urad dal into a smooth paste with water.
  3. Mix with rice flour and let the batter ferment overnight.
  4. Cook on a flat pan until crispy and golden brown.

Sandwich

Ingredients: Bread, Lettuce, Tomato, Cheese, Mayonnaise.

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread slices lightly.
  2. Spread mayonnaise on one side of each slice.
  3. Layer the bread with lettuce, tomato, and cheese.
  4. Top with another slice of bread and serve.
